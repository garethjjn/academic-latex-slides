## Commentary (≤ 5 sentences)

22-FW is a tax-and-restatement nexus paper whose intro is closer to the canonical 5-block structure than the 19-JWW / 22-LNS counter-templates but diverges from the modern JAE norm in two diagnostic ways. Block 1 opens with an M1A lit-baseline framed as paucity-of-insight ("Research provides limited insight..."), states the M3 research question by sentence four of ¶1, and reinforces motivation with a policy/resource-constraint anchor in ¶2 before delivering the formal M2 direct gap in ¶3; the gap thus appears in Block 1 ¶3 rather than the more typical ¶1-end placement. Block 2 substitutes a three-reasons enumeration plus a cockroach-theory anchor for the canonical theory derivation, and notably omits an explicit M4 tension paragraph in the intro (tension is deferred to §2.3 hypothesis development, where the "reasons to expect restatements might not draw much notice" paragraph appears). The Final block carries three contributions plus an M7 closing limitation paragraph; the contributions are framed as adding to two distinct literatures (IRS-public-info use and restatement-stakeholder costs) plus the broader book-tax-aggressiveness debate — a dual-literature closer consistent with the paper's tax-restatement nexus positioning.

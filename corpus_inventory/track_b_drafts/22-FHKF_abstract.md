## Commentary

This abstract follows a descriptive-to-empirical arc rather than the strict 5-move JAE template. It opens with a direct research question, immediately introduces a novel resume dataset, and provides workforce demographics before stating the main results. The abstract explicitly reports economic magnitudes for quality, fees, and labor displacement, which is atypical for exploratory papers but strengthens the empirical pitch. The qualitative interview component serves as a corroboration block, validating the archival findings and clarifying managerial intent. The absence of a formal "prediction-with-tension" sentence and a "Collectively/Overall" closer reflects a more direct, results-forward style common in accounting technology papers.

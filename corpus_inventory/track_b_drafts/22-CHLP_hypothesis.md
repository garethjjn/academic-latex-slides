The anchor sequence is a corpus-rare four-prior parallelism (home bias, peer networks, board ties, analyst ties), each prior glossed with the same "implying that information [is] transmitted / ﬂows" verb cadence — the rhetorical purpose is to bracket the focal auditor-tie hypothesis as a natural extension of an already-established multi-channel literature. Tension uses an unenumerated four-mechanism build (ethical rules → firm controls → risk aversion → reputation), foreshadowing the intro's bracketed-priors anti-AI tell preserved in JAR style. The "Hypothesis 1:" inline label (older 07-DHT style) and absence of "ceteris paribus" mark this as the pre-modern hypothesis-statement format (per `hypothesis_patterns.md` quick-reference table — closer to 07-DHT than 26-KLYY).

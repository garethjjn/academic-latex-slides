## Commentary
The results section of 22-CHLP follows a three-part structure (4.1 baseline, 4.2 sensitivity, 4.3 endogeneity) that embeds many of the canonical moves. The baseline block includes descriptive statistics, a coefficient table, a primary‑result lead, an economic‑magnitude translation, and a brief description of fixed‑effects identification. The sensitivity block walks through robustness panels while incorporating alternative measures, a cross‑sectional split by university type, a channel‑based falsification (excluding manager-executive ties), and an explicit null‑result handling. The endogeneity block uses mandatory auditor rotation as an identification shock, though the paper does not run a full falsification test in the sense of a placebo. The section closes by returning to the prediction. No separate mechanism subsection appears, but several channel‑ruling tests serve that function within the sensitivity block.

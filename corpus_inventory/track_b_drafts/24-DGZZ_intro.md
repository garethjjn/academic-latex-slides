## Commentary (≤ 5 sentences)

24-DGZZ follows the canonical 5-block intro arc but with two structural deviations: (1) the M3 research-question statement is split across the Block-1 opener and the Block-3 setting paragraph ("This setting allows us to study..." + "We extend existing international research..."), rather than appearing as a clean last-sentence-of-Block-1 form; and (2) Block 3 sample-size content is back-loaded into the Block-4 main-finding paragraph rather than presented in a dedicated setting block. Block 2 develops an unusually long four-mechanism walk (firms' benefit-cost tradeoff · entering-bank information asymmetry · entering-bank lending-skill substitution · delegated-monitoring by banks) with the M4 tension marker ("It is unclear ex ante") placed at the head of the mechanism walk rather than at the end. Block 4 uses the canonical "consistent with" lead and reports F1 percentage-point magnitudes on a Big-N-share DV (1.8%) and an industry-expert DV (2.1%), explicitly paired with F2 percent-of-unconditional-base form (2.1% / 7.7% of base) — the F1+F2 combination that the corpus prefers. Final block carries two prose-numbered contributions (causal link via single-country setting · audit-debt-financing reverse-direction framing) without the closing-limitation paragraph that 4/6 of the corpus uses.

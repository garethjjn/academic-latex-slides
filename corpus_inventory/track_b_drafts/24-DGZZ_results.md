## Commentary
The extracted results section contains only the primary (main) results table and its accompanying narrative. It opens directly with the primary result lead, making it an Archetype‑A sample. The moves present are heavily concentrated on coefficient reporting, economic magnitude translation (both absolute and relative to the unconditional mean, plus a benchmark against SIZE), a walk through the table's columns and panels, a transition to an alternative dependent variable and an alternative treatment variable, control-variable corroboration, and a final closing summary. Because the paper separates descriptive statistics into an earlier section (Table 2), the results section itself contains no descriptive-statistics sub-section, which is consistent with several corpus papers that place descriptives before the main empirical analysis.

## 5. Additional Analyses

Our main results establish that sharing a Big 4 auditor increases the pairwise comparability of reported earnings within an auditor's clientele. A natural concern is that auditor choice is endogenous: firms with similar fundamentals, and hence mechanically similar earnings, may sort into the same auditor for reasons unrelated to a shared audit style.
